21 Mind-Blowing Journaling Power Prompts for Living Deep

This workbook contains 21 powerful journaling prompts designed to take you on a profound journey of self-discovery, helping you to "live deep" and connect with your most authentic self. Each prompt includes questions for you to answer, space to write your responses, and instructions for using AI to gain deeper insights into your answers.

How to Use This Interactive Workbook

  • Choose a prompt that resonates with you
  • Answer all the questions in the space provided
  • Copy your answers and the prompt instructions into an AI chat (like ChatGPT)
  • Review the AI's analysis of your responses for deeper insights
  • Complete the recommended journaling exercise
  • Continue with additional prompts as desired
  • When you've completed all prompts, compile all your answers and AI analyses for a comprehensive life review
